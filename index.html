About

Odesza is an American electronic music duo originating from Washington. It consists of Harrison Mills and Clayton Knight, known individually as Catacombkid and BeachesBeaches. They formed in 2012, shortly before Mills and Knight graduated from Western Washington University. Additionally, Odesza is composed of a complete brass section, drum line, and singers including Naomi Wild and WYNNE.

Why do I like ODESZA?

ODESZA was, no doubt, my gateway band to the Indie genre in general. My favourite style of music, since I was a kid, is Electronic Music. I would spend whole afternoons in Soundcloud trying to discover new music, new artists, like Kygo, The Chainsmokers, between others back then, when, in 2014 I discovered ODESZA.
With them, my perception of music changed, it was more organic, almost orchestral. And with them, more artists and genres came, at least for me. I could say it changed my life, musically speaking. Started paying more attention to the sounds, to the way and way some music is built that way.
